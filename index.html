Game Play

Unleash your creativity and master the art of disguise. SpyClue is the easiest game you can play in a group, anywhere and anytime. It takes only a few minutes to learn. There are two words carrying similar meaning in each round, and players will be randomly assigned one of the two words. This game challenges you to identify your team mates as fast as you can and work together with them to expose the other group. Be careful though, don't fall into your opponent's trap.

Interacts Between People

SpyClue is all about quality time over screen time. The game is all about interactions between people and that's where all the fun comes from! Get a group of friends and start playing, and be prepared to laugh, A WHOLE LOT.
